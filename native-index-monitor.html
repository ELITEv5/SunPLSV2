Can SunPLS become PulseChain's native monetary unit?

This page is a live theoretical test — running a new purchasing power oracle alongside the deployed SunPLS protocol to see if they could work together one day. No code has changed. Nothing is wired together yet. We are just watching the data.

What is SunPLS

SunPLS is a WPLS-backed CDP protocol on PulseChain. Lock WPLS as collateral, mint SunPLS. A stability fee and redemption mechanism keeps the market price close to the redemption price R — a target the market is expected to chase, not the other way around. The protocol is live, immutable, and fully on-chain.

What is the Native Index

A new on-chain oracle that tracks a basket of PulseChain-native assets, all denominated in WPLS. It starts at 1.0× and is capped at 1% movement per update — slow and deliberate, resistant to manipulation. Every price comes from PulseX V1 on-chain liquidity. No Chainlink. No external feeds. The chain prices itself.

The Hypothesis

What if R tracked the index? As PulseChain grows — more usage, stronger native tokens, rising gas demand — the index rises, and R rises with it. SunPLS holders would not hold a static peg. They would hold a claim on PulseChain's own purchasing power, appreciating slowly as the ecosystem expands.

This is fundamentally different from every stablecoin today. Not pegged to USD. Not pegged to gold. Not algorithmic in an unstable way. Pegged to the chain itself — and still fully collateralised and redeemable at all times.

What we're watching

  • Does the index move in ways that are economically meaningful — tracking real PulseChain activity rather than noise?
  • Does the P / Native R ratio stay close to 100%? That would mean the market would naturally find equilibrium under this regime.
  • Are borrowers and holders not destabilised by slow, capped R movement?

What success looks like

Over weeks and months the index drifts smoothly. The P / Native R ratio behaves like the current P / R ratio — healthy, mean-reverting, close to par. If that holds, Controller v2 wires the index in as a parallel deploy alongside the live protocol. SunPLS becomes the first CDP asset with a monetary policy set by the chain it lives on.
