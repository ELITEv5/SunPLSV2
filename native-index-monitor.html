SunPLS · Native Index Monitor

Can SunPLS become PulseChain's native monetary unit?

This page is a live theoretical test — running a new purchasing power oracle alongside the deployed SunPLS protocol to see if they could work together. No code has changed. Nothing is wired together yet. We are just watching.

What is SunPLS

SunPLS is a WPLS-backed CDP protocol on PulseChain. Lock WPLS as collateral, mint SunPLS. A stability fee and redemption mechanism keeps the market price close to the redemption price R — a target the market is expected to chase, not the other way around. The protocol is live, immutable, and fully on-chain.

What is the Native Index

A new on-chain oracle tracking a basket of PulseChain-native assets denominated in WPLS. It starts at 1.0× and moves at most 1% per update — slow and deliberate, resistant to manipulation. The basket reflects genuine PulseChain economic activity: token prices, usage, and gas demand.

The Hypothesis

What if R tracked the index? As PulseChain grows — more usage, stronger native tokens, rising gas demand — the index rises, and R rises with it. SunPLS holders would not hold a static peg. They would hold a claim on PulseChain's own purchasing power, appreciating slowly as the ecosystem expands.

△

What we're watching. The Theoretical Native Target below shows what R would be today if it had always tracked the index. The P / Native R ratio shows whether SunPLS would be above or below par under this regime — and whether that looks healthy or not.

△

What success looks like. Over weeks and months: the index moves smoothly and in economically meaningful ways. The P / Native R ratio stays close to 100%. Borrowers and holders are not destabilised. If that holds, Controller v2 wires it in — and SunPLS becomes the first CDP asset pegged to a chain rather than a fiat currency.

△

No USD. No TradFi oracle. Every price in this system comes from PulseX V1 on-chain liquidity. There is no Chainlink feed, no external data source, no dependency on anything outside PulseChain. The index is endogenous — the chain prices itself.

△

Still a CDP. Still safe. The stability fee, liquidation mechanics, stability pool, and collateral ratios do not change. SunPLS remains fully collateralised and redeemable at R at all times. The only change is that R itself has a direction — it can drift upward with the chain, not just sit still.
